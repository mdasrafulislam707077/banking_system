Transfer VAT Rules

Our bank offers personalized VAT rates based on the type of account you hold. Below are the VAT rules applicable to personal and business accounts.

Personal Account VAT Rates:
  1. Under $1,000: 5% VAT
  2. $1,000 to $5,000: 3% VAT
  3. Above $5,000: 1.5% VAT
Business Account VAT Rates:
  1. No VAT charges: Business accounts enjoy full exemption from VAT.
  2. Free transactions: All transactions within business accounts are free of VAT, providing cost-efficiency for business operations.
